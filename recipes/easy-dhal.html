Easy Dhal Makhani

Easy and delicious vegan eating right here! A simple bowl of Dal Makhani, loaded with Coriander! Spicy and tomatoey, it really is a delight for the taste buds and its nutritious too.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon fenugreek
  • 2â€“3 tablespoons garam masala
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red chilli or 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1/2 cup lentils
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tin kidney beans, drained
  • salt, pepper to taste
  • Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium sized pan or pot, add the coconut oil and then add the garlic, onions, chilli and spices.
  2. Fry for 2-3 minutes on medium heat, until onions are soft and translucent.
  3. Add lentils and water and tomato paste.
  4. Allow to cook for 20 minutes or so, then add the kidney beans and coconut milk.
  5. Stir well and allow to cook for another 5-10 mins.
  6. Serve with rice and lots of fresh coriander.
